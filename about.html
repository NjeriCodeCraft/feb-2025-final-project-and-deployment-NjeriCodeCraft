Our Story

NovelNest was born from a shared passion for literature and the desire to create a welcoming space for book lovers. Founded in 2020 by a group of avid readers, our mission is to help you discover your next favorite book.

We believe that every book has its reader and every reader has their book. Our team carefully curates recommendations across all genres to help you find stories that will stay with you long after you've turned the last page.
